Sound Design

Seven years of professional sound design and music composition for films, visual media, and commercial clients including Adidas.

The work spans spatial audio design, real-time sound processing, field recording, and generative sound systems. Projects include cinematic soundscapes, interactive audio for installations, branded content, and experimental music composition.

Using tools like Pro Tools, Ableton Live, and Pure Data, the work emphasizes both technical precision and creative experimentation—building custom processing chains, designing unique sound palettes, and creating immersive audio experiences that enhance visual storytelling.
