Vertigo Installations

Interactive

Interactive artworks and installations built during internship at Vertigo, a creative studio focused on experiential design and interactive art.

The work involved building custom interactive systems using Raspberry Pi, Arduino, and microcontrollers. Projects included custom LED fixtures, lighting control systems, and TouchDesigner-based generative visuals that responded to audience interaction or environmental input.

The installations combined software development with physical computing—writing control code, designing circuit layouts, and integrating sensors and actuators to create immersive, responsive environments for galleries and public spaces.

Technologies / Tools

Raspberry Pi, TouchDesigner, Arduino, LEDs, Physical Computing, Interactive Systems
